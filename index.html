What is GDG?
Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google’s developer technology; everything from the Android, Chrome, Drive, and Google Cloud platforms, to product APIs like the Cast API,Maps API, and YouTube API. A GDG can take many forms — from just a few people getting together to watch our latest video, to large gatherings with demos and tech talks, to events like code sprints and hackathons.
Vision
We encourage and invite Technology Enthusiasts/ Developers/Designers /Interested Students/Professionals to be a part of this awesome community of like minded personalities and come up with innovative and futuristic product ideas, that is set to re-define the digital culture and people’s lives.
#WomenTechMakers
Google's Women Techmakers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology.
